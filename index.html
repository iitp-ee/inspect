About INSPECT 2026

INSPECT 2026 marks the third edition of the IEEE INSPECT Conference Series, providing a premier international platform for researchers, academicians, industry professionals, and students to present and exchange innovative research in emerging areas of science, engineering, and technology.

Event Highlights:

  • International keynote speeches from industry experts
  • Technical workshops and hands-on sessions
  • Poster and oral presentations by researchers
  • Networking opportunities with global participants
  • Cutting-edge research discussions and collaborations

The first and second editions of the IEEE INSPECT conference series were successfully hosted at Atal Bihari Vajpayee-Indian Institute of Information Technology and Management (ABV-IIITM), Gwalior, establishing a strong academic and organizational foundation for the series.

The first edition, IEEE INSPECT 2024, received 550 paper submissions, out of which 165 papers were selected through a rigorous peer-review process, reflecting the conference's commitment to quality and selectivity. The proceedings are published in IEEE Xplore and are available at:https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/servlet/opac?punumber=10895986

Building on this success, the second edition, IEEE INSPECT 2025, attracted 1,050 submissions, with 211 papers accepted after rigorous review, demonstrating the growing international recognition of INSPECT. The proceedings are available in IEEE Xplore at: https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/servlet/opac?punumber=11349708

Continuing this strong legacy, INSPECT 2026 is scheduled to be held at the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Patna, aiming to further strengthen interdisciplinary research, innovation, and global collaboration while upholding IEEE's standards of technical excellence and ethical publications.

The conference is technically co-sponsored by the IEEE Kolkata Section.

Accepted papers will be submitted for inclusion into IEEE Xplore subject to meeting IEEE Xplore's scope and quality requirements.

About IIT Patna

Excellence in Education and Research

Indian Institute of Technology Patna

Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Patna is one of the new IITs established by an Act of the Indian Parliament on August 06, 2008. IIT Patna has ten departments: These are Computer Science & Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Chemical and Biochemical Engineering, Civil & Environmental Engineering, Materials Science & Engineering, Chemistry, Physics, Mathematics and Humanities & Social Science departments. IIT Patna campus is located at Bihta which is approximately 30 kms from Patna city.

About Patna

The City of Spirituality and History

Historical Significance

Patna, one of the oldest inhabited cities in the world, holds immense historical and spiritual significance. Known as Patliputra in ancient times, it was the capital of the Maurya and Gupta empires, witnessing the rise of Buddhism and housing the great Nalanda University.

The city is home to the Mahabodhi Temple, one of the oldest brick temples in the world, and the Harmandir Sahib, a sacred pilgrimage site for Sikhs. Today, Patna blends its rich heritage with modern development, making it an ideal destination for conferences and academic gatherings.

With excellent connectivity via rail, air, and road, Patna welcomes visitors from around the world. Experience the confluence of spirituality, culture, and modernity during your visit.
